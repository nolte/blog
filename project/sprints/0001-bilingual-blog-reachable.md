## Goal

At the close of this sprint, an outside reader who lands on the deployed
GitHub Pages URL finds a working bilingual blog: an English landing page
with at least one post, a German counterpart linked from the language
switch, and a transparent indication that the post was AI-drafted and
human-curated. The MVP coverage spans the four pulled roadmap items:
the site is live (R-1), real portfolio-derived posts exist (R-2),
posts are findable by tag and topic (R-3), and the AI-disclosure UX is
visible (R-4). The value-verifying feature anchors on R-1 — the
narrowest reading of "the bilingual blog is reachable".

# Vision

A bilingual blog and personal knowledge base that turns the author's
software-project work into durable English / German posts — built to serve
technical readers passing through, portfolio reviewers evaluating the
author, and the author's own future-self equally well, with AI-drafted
content kept transparently distinct from hand-curated content.

## Outcomes

Outcomes are end-user benefits, each tied to an audience identifier defined
in [AUDIENCES.md](../AUDIENCES.md). Outcome identifiers (`O-<n>`) are
monotonic and never reused across the project's lifetime, even after
deletion.

- **O-1** — Technical readers and portfolio reviewers reach every post in
  their preferred language (EN / DE) without translation drift between the
  two. *(audience: A, B)*
- **O-2** — Portfolio reviewers form a clear picture of the author's
  projects and working style within the first visit. *(audience: B)*
- **O-3** — The author re-finds his own past explanations via tag and topic
  indices when revisiting old work. *(audience: C)*
- **O-4** — Readers see clearly which posts are AI-drafted and trust both
  AI-drafted and hand-curated content. *(audience: A, B, L)*
- **O-5** — Portfolio-project work surfaces as blog posts as a routine part
  of the project lifecycle, not as an extra chore. *(audience: B, C, D)*

## English

The three specifications below moved to
[`nolte/claude-shared`](https://github.com/nolte/claude-shared) on 2026-05-28 and
were generalised there. `nolte/blog` is now a "reference consumer" of those
specs rather than their owner. The `nolte-shared:blog-author` skill still authors
post pairs into this repo exactly as before; only the contract documents moved.

This signpost can be removed after one release cycle once links elsewhere have
caught up.
